Kingdom Got Talent


This program is designed for children and young adults in the Christian community to provide them with the opportunity to showcase their God given talent and creative skills. Very often young adults and children of Faith find it difficult taking part in Talent Hunt showcases in the society because they are afraid of being labelled unchristian. To break this barrier and provide opportunity for such Christians. KINGDOM GOT TALENT â€œ has been put in place to fulfill the yearning of such Christians. The program KINGDOM GOT TALENT has also been set up into categories, and the reason is to make it inclusive of most age groups. The categories are:

1. 6-9
2. 10-13
3. 14-17
4. 18-21

Areas of participation include: Drama, Song, Comedy, Instrumental, Painting, Poetry, and Rapping.
Each Category is free to participate in any of the groups.
This gives person within the age range to participate.
